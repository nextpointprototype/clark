CEO Message

At NextPoint Group, we provide essential services and solutions for national defense, intelligence and homeland security.

"This focus has made NextPoint Group a trusted national partner, ever vigilant in serving the critical interests of our nation with honesty and integrity."

NextPoint Group is there with intelligence and national security solutions, helping our clients generate actionable information to protect our warfighters and secure our homeland. We work side by side with clients throughout the federal government, delivering the innovative professional services and IT solutions they need to solve their most complex problems.

The key to providing these solutions is people. NextPoint Group believes that this principle is vital to success in any consulting organization. NextPoint Group is a people organization first and foremost. We strive to keep our small company feel by creating a family-like culture for our employees, encouraging communication, rewarding performance, fostering development and having fun. We believe motivated and happy employees equal ecstatic clients.

The sourcing of highly qualified and motivated consultants is only half of the equation. Retention is key and we regard this as a core value of NextPoint. We believe employee satisfaction to be the most important factor to the continued success of the firm.We have exciting capabilities to offer. Join us in achieving our vision of being the best in serving our country’s vital national missions!

~ Ron Kattas
