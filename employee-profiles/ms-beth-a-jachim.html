Ms. Beth A. Jachim

Managing Partner

As one of NextPoint Groups Partners, Ms. Jachim works with her Partners and Senior Management to ensure the company is aligned for market competitiveness while maintaining compliance and strategic growth within the federal consulting arena. Currently NextPoint Group generates $30M in annual revenue with 150 employees based in six countries.

Ms. Jachim works with her team and partners to ensure the company is aligned for market competitiveness while maintaining compliance and strategic growth within the federal consulting arena. Currently NextPoint Group generates $30M in annual revenue with 150 employees based in six countries.

Ms. Jachim also leads the company’s charitable involvement. NextPoint Group was the major event sponsor for all five years of Mini’s Mission kickball tournament which partnered with KickIt and Jeff Gordon’s Children’s Foundation to raise awareness and money for children’s cancer. NextPoint Group is also a second year Silver Sponsor of the Dulles Plane Pull benefitting The Special Olympics. Additionally, NextPoint Group sponsors the annual Willing Warriors Gala and also participates in sponsoring employees and their families in charities of their choice. In 2019 NextPoint Group organized its first golf tournament which raised over $10,000.00 benefitting one of its employee’s children’s foundations, Joey’s Foundation, which raises awareness and money to support children with brain injuries.

Ms. Jachim has over twenty years of experience providing operational direction in both commercial and governmental industries. She has worked for Big Five consulting and start-up technology companies as well as the Departments of Defense and Commerce.

Previously, Ms. Jachim managed the operations of two start-up IT companies. In 2000 she was the second employee of Inteliguard, a consulting firm and SunMicrosystems partner, where she served as Manager of Operations. After one year the company was generating over $1M in revenue and in 2001 merged with T4 Consulting Group. In 2005 she assisted in the expansive growth and success of Codin Solutions, an IT Intelligence services company where she served as Director of Operations.

In addition Ms. Jachim provided consulting services to WinStar Communciations where she managed the 24 × 7 operations of WinStar’s 168 switches and hubs in seventeen major U.S. cities. Ms. Jachim also spent two years with EDS’s Government Consulting Group where she provided Change Management services to the U.S. Mint during their state quarter initiative. With the Department of Defenses’ Defense Civilian Personnel Management Service she was part of a team that supplied research and materials published in the National Performance Review.

Ms. Jachim is a graduate of Old Dominion University.
