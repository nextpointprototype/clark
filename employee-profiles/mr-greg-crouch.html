Mr. Greg Crouch

Vice President

Mr. Crouch has over 14 years of experience providing development, implementation, deployment and operation of information systems and technology solutions for business needs. He has experience planning, directing and coordinating all activities associated with creating software across multiple functional areas and products.

Mr. Crouch joined NextPoint Group in April 2010 as a Program Manager. Prior to that, he held various management positions with ITSolutions, Perot Systems, and BAE Systems.

Mr. Crouch is a graduate from George Mason University and holds a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology with a specialization in Networking and Security.
