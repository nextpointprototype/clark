Mr. Shawn Potter

Director

Mr. Potter has over 17 years of experience designing, developing, and delivering a variety of multimedia solutions including interactive web-based training, CMS websites, video production, 3D animation, photography, and web and print design. He has proven experience translating client concepts into creative, strategic solutions and directing and coordinating the necessary activities to ensure a successful end product.

Mr. Potter has received numerous awards for his work, including the Intelligence Community Media Excellence Award.

Mr. Potter joined NextPoint Group in July of 2012 as the Head of the Multimedia Group. Prior to that, he held various management positions with Dell Services Federal Government and Perot Systems Government Services.

Mr. Potter is a graduate from James Madison University and holds a Bachelor of Fine Arts with a concentration in 3D computer animation.
