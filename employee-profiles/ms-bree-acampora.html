Ms. Bree Acampora

Director

Ms. Acampora, CMP, brings over 17 years of diverse leadership experience in operations, business development, program support and team development primarily in the meetings and service industry. She has served on executive committees with Hilton, Marriott and Independent Luxury Brands providing strategic and tactical guidance to drive optimal business outcomes and exceptional results for customers.

Her scope also included facilities & asset management, vendor engagement and capital projects. She has led several technology implementations and has brought that expertise to NextPoint Group, integrating and improving HRIS systems with a focus on user experience.

Joining the NextPoint team in 2015, Ms. Acampora now leads all Human Resources functions including new employee on-boarding, employee relations, special events, benefit administration, compliance and the company’s continuing education program.

Ms. Acampora is a Summa Cum Laude graduate of The University of North Carolina at Greensboro with a B.S. in Organizational Communication with a focus on Interpersonal Relations in the Workplace.
